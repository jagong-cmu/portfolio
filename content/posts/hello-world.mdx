This is my first blog post. I built this site with Next.js, Tailwind v4, and a
small design-token system, and I write the posts in MDX.

## What I learned

You can write **Markdown** here — and because it's MDX, React components too.
The frontmatter at the top is validated against a schema, so a post with a
missing title or a bad date fails the build instead of shipping broken.

A few things I wanted from day one:

- Posts are just files. No CMS to log into, no database for words.
- Type-safe content. The `title`, `date`, and `excerpt` are checked at build.
- Fast pages. Each post is a static server-rendered route.
